2. Types of Edits

Our chatbot is capable of identifying common objects in images. When describing objects, please refer to the object as a whole (e.g., the man), instead of parts of the object (e.g., the man's eyebrows)

Our chatbot can modify 5 attributes (1) brightness (2) contrast (3) hue (4) saturation (5) lightness by a value (-100 to 100), which are shown below the image.
